How it works

Three steps.
That's the whole thing.

Captrieve is not a notes app. It's not a reminder app. It doesn't want your attention. It wants to disappear into your life and show up when something matters.

1

Capture it

Tap, speak, done – before the thought is gone. Voice is primary because thoughts move faster than typing. Photo and text work too, for when the moment calls for it.

2

Tell it when or where

Set a time, a date, a place, or a combination. When you leave work. When you arrive at the clinic. Tomorrow morning. When you land. The next time you walk into the grocery store.

3

It finds you

At the right moment, in the right place, it tells you what you need to know. Not in a pile of notes you have to dig through. Not because an algorithm decided you were ready. Because you told it to.

Who is Captrieve for?
Anyone whose thoughts arrive at the wrong moment.

Creative people

Ideas don't wait for a convenient moment. Captrieve captures them at the speed they arrive and delivers them back when you are positioned to do something with them – at the studio, at the desk, in the flow state that makes the work happen.

People with ADHD

A reminder that fires at an arbitrary time is easy to dismiss and forget. A reminder that fires when you walk through the door of the place where it matters is a different thing entirely. Context-triggered retrieval is not a convenience for people with ADHD – it is a meaningful functional accommodation.

People managing their health and memory

The question you thought of on Tuesday needs to be there on Thursday when the doctor asks if you have anything else. Captrieve is a reliable external memory that reminds you in exactly the context where they are useful – and everything stays private on your phone, always.
