The thought you have now is the thought future you will need.

Captrieve solves a single problem: You have a thought you do not want to lose, you cannot act on it right now, and you want to remember it when you can put it to use.

You remember to do something – when you can't do anything about it, so you have to remember it again at the right time.

You need to pick up that thing at the store, say something just that way when you have that tough conversation, or that sales conversation.

You have to work that idea to a character's backstory, put that riff in that song, finally remember to make that call, do that task, … and you are in the car right now, driving. You are in bed right now, and instead of having a million unfinished things streaming through your head, escaping almost as quickly as they arrive, you just want to go to sleep but not forget them!

So you hope you will remember these things when you can act on them. How's that working for you?

In desperation, you dictate it to Siri or Alexa to put in a note, and forget to ask for it later, or you put it in a Google doc, or even a spreadsheet, and it sits there, passively not reminding you. You put it into a reminder and if you happen to know it will come in handy at 4 o'clock on Wednesday, you have the one scenario that's genuinely useful.

What if your ideas came looking for you the next time you are at your computer, the next time you are leaving work to go home, the next time you you are in the kitchen, the next time you meet with that client, the next time you are at a doctor appointment, or just before you give that speech? Wow. That's a whole different ballgame.

How it works

Three steps.
That's the whole thing.

Captrieve is not a notes app. It is not a reminder app. It does not want your attention. You capture a thought when you have it, and it show up when you need it.

1

Capture it

Tap, speak, done — before the thought is gone. Voice is primary because thoughts move faster than typing. Photo and text work too, for when the moment calls for it.

2

Tell it when or where

Set a cue based on time, proximity to a named WiFi network, your car, your home, your job, or 20 minutes after leaving work, or 15 minutes before arriving at home, or the next time you are at your computer, or when your flight lands, or when you get a minute to play your guitar, or just before you go to bed, or as soon as you wake up, or …

3

It finds you

At the right moment, in the right place, it surfaces. Not in a pile of notes you have to dig through. Not because an algorithm decided you were ready. Because you told it when you would need it.

Who is Captrieve for?

People who forget things (everyone)

  • "Why can't I remember to make that call when I am at home in the evening?"
  • "Oh yeah, I promised I would go to Laura's game on Saturday, and to Michael's play next Friday. I have to put those things in my calendar when I get home."
  • "I must tell Klaus about the new direction I want to take when we meet with the Board."
  • "I can't forget again! We're out of triple-A batteries, and low on toilet paper. Stop at Target on the way home."

Creative people

Inspiration strikes when it strikes. Captrieve captures them at the speed they arrive and delivers them back when you are positioned to make use of them — at the studio, at the desk, in the flow state that makes the work happen.

People with ADHD

A reminder that fires at an arbitrary time is easy to dismiss and forget. A reminder that fires when you walk through the door of the place where it matters is a different thing entirely. Context-triggered retrieval is not a convenience for people with ADHD — it is a meaningful quality of life upgrade.

People managing their health and memory

The question you thought of on Tuesday needs to be there t your appointment on Thursday when the doctor asks if you have anything else to discuss. Captrieve is a reliable external memory that surfaces things in exactly the context where they are needed – and everything stays private, right on your own phone, unshared in the Free and Solo modes, and only shared with the specific people you choose on the Networked plan. On the Free and Solo plans, nothing ever even goes to our servers, and on the Network plan everything personal is encrypted to government security standards.

Families and caregivers

Set Captrieve up for someone you care for. Know they are moving through their day, or know to call them if they are not. Share a thought that finds them at exactly the right moment. Help them remember their medications, and their appointments. Learn more →
